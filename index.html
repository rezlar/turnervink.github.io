Stuff I've Made

Square

Packed with features, Square is a simple watchface that can suit anyone's needs! Pick any colour combination, have the middle bar display the battery level, have a weather forecast shown when you flick your wrist, all the time, or not at all, and choose your preferred date format. Square also has support for multiple languages, currently English, French, Spanish, and German.



Material Ribbon

Two crossed "ribbons" show the weather, battery level, and Bluetooth status with beautiful Material icons. Choose from 6 colours schemes and follow the sdaow of the horizontal ribbbon as you progress towards your daily step goal.



Vacation Time

For when the hours don't matter! See the progress through the day in 8 easy to read stages on a background colour-matched to the current time. Shake to view remaining battery and current "feels-like" temperature and hourly forecast.



Undertime

Checking the time... it fills you with DETERMINATION. A new Undertale character every hour or every time you launch the watchface. The health bar empties if your watch disconnects from your phone, and the LV shows the current temperature.
